Description

 


Sensor4Cities is a tool to disseminate scalar/multimedia data for single or groups of users, to smart cities applications. The tool allows users to access and share data in a single, user-friendly and efficient manner through the use of social networks, web sites, including mobile version of it. Sensor4Cities was developed by the Federal University of Pará, Belém - Brazil with collaboration of Federal Univesity of Minas Gerais, Belo Horizonte - Brazil and University of Bern, Bern - Switzerland.
